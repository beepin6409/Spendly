Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken, used solely to improve the app.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and reports.
  • To send essential service communications such as password resets.
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues in the app.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply industry-standard measures — including encrypted connections and access controls — to protect it from unauthorised access, alteration, or loss.

You can delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use a small number of trusted third-party services to help deliver the app (for example, hosting providers). These services are contractually bound to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify. We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing or analytics.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Export your expense data at any time from your account settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channels. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
